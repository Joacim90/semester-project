Space Day at the Community Science Museum

A Galactic Adventure for Curious Minds!

Get ready to launch into a universe of discovery at Space Day, a full-day celebration of all things outer space! Specially designed for kids aged 7 to 15, this event invites young explorers and their families to uncover the wonders of our solar system and beyond through interactive exhibits, hands-on experiments, and out-of-this-world fun. Whether your child dreams of becoming an astronaut, is fascinated by black holes, or just loves to look up at the stars—Space Day will ignite their imagination and fuel a lifelong love for science.

What's in Store on Space Day?

  • Astronaut Training Camp: Suit up! Kids can experience what it’s like to prepare for space missions with physical and mental challenges inspired by real astronaut training. Learn how astronauts eat, sleep, and stay strong in zero gravity!
  • Live Rocket Launch Demonstrations: Watch in awe as we launch small-scale rockets and explain the exciting science behind thrust, fuel, and flight. Want to build your own? Join a mini workshop and design a safe, air-powered rocket to take home!
  • Planetarium Star Shows: Step inside our immersive planetarium and journey through the stars. Explore planets, moons, constellations, and far-off galaxies in high-definition projections guided by real astronomers.
  • Meet a Space Scientist:Ever wonder what it’s like to work with NASA or study the stars? Now’s your chance! Talk to real-life space scientists and engineers, hear their amazing stories, and ask all your burning questions.
  • Mission Control STEM Zone: Dive into coding, robotics, and space tech with hands-on stations where kids can control rovers, simulate space missions, and explore satellite technology.
  • Build Your Own Spacecraft Craft Corner: Get creative with recycled materials and craft supplies to build your very own rocket, rover, or alien spaceship. Take your creation home as a souvenir from your mission!

Why Attend Space Day?

  • Ignite curiosity about the universe in a fun, engaging environment.
  • Encourage STEM learning through play, experimentation, and real-world science.
  • Connect with scientists, educators, and other space-loving kids.li>
  • Make lasting memories through exciting, educational experiences.

    • This event is perfect for families, school groups, and young science fans of all levels of knowledge and interest!
