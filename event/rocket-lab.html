Rocket Lab

Unleash Your Inner Scientist!

Welcome to Rocket Lab, an action-packed museum activity designed for future engineers and space enthusiasts aged 7–15. Get ready to blast off into the world of rocketry with hands-on building projects, exciting launches, and interactive lessons that explore the science of flight and space exploration.

What to Expect

Rocket Lab offers an out-of-this-world lineup of experiences, including:

  • Build-Your-Own Rockets: Design, construct, and launch mini-rockets using safe, kid-friendly materials.
  • Flight Simulations: Step into the pilot's seat with digital simulations that mimic real-life space missions.
  • STEM Stations: Discover the physics behind propulsion, aerodynamics, and orbital motion.
  • Team Missions: Work in groups to solve engineering challenges and launch the most successful rocket.

Why Join Us?

Participating in Rocket Lab will help kids:

  • Learn fundamental principles of physics and engineering.
  • Develop teamwork and problem-solving abilities.
  • Explore real-world applications of STEM subjects.
  • Have a blast while learning about space and rockets!
