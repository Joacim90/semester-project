Dino World

Step Into the Land of Giants!

Welcome to Dino World, an exciting museum activity designed for kids aged 7–15. Travel back in time to the age of dinosaurs with hands-on discoveries, fossil fun, and thrilling exhibits that bring prehistoric creatures to life.

What to Expect

Dino World offers a variety of roaring-good activities, including:

  • Fossil Digs: Unearth replica fossils and learn how paleontologists uncover the secrets of the past.
  • Dino ID Stations: Discover fascinating facts about different dinosaur species, their habitats, and how they lived.
  • Dino Science Labs: Explore the ancient Earth with interactive stations on volcanoes, extinction events, and more.
  • Prehistoric Puzzles:Solve dino-themed challenges and work together to complete fossil reconstructions.

Why Join Us?

Participating in Dino World will help kids:

  • Develop observational and investigative skills.
  • Learn about Earth's history and evolution.
  • Get hands-on with real scientific methods.
  • Experience a fun, educational adventure with fellow dino lovers!
