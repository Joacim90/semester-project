The Human Body

Explore the Amazing Machine Inside You! !

Welcome to The Human Body exhibit—an exciting and interactive experience designed for kids aged 7–15. Discover how your heart beats, your brain thinks, your lungs breathe, and your muscles move in this hands-on journey through the most incredible machine on Earth: YOU!

What to Expect

At the Human Body exhibit, kids will explore:

  • Life-Size Body Models: See and interact with detailed models of organs, bones, and systems that show how the body works together.
  • Interactive Stations: Test your reflexes, check your pulse, assemble a skeleton, and explore how the senses function.
  • Hands-On Labs: Try simple experiments to learn about digestion, blood flow, and even how germs spread and can be prevented.
  • Body in Motion Zone: Jump, stretch, and move to learn how muscles, joints, and bones help us stay active and strong.

Why It’s Awesome

Through this exhibit, kids will:

  • Understand how their bodies function and how to take care of them.
  • Get curious about health, biology, and anatomy.
  • Explore real science through movement, play, and discovery.
  • Build awareness of nutrition, fitness, and hygiene.
