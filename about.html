About the Community Science Museum

Where Curiosity Comes to Life

Welcome to the Community Science Museum, a vibrant and inclusive space where science, discovery, and imagination come together! Located in the heart of the city, our mission is to inspire young minds, empower future innovators, and make science accessible to everyone.

Whether you're a curious child, a lifelong learner, or a family looking for a fun and educational outing, the Community Science Museum offers something for all ages.

What We Do

At the Community Science Museum, we believe that learning should be exciting, interactive, and hands-on. That’s why our exhibits, events, and programs are designed to engage, educate, and entertain.

  • Interactive Exhibits – Explore physics, chemistry, biology, technology, astronomy, and more through fully interactive displays.
  • STEM Workshops & Labs – Take part in creative workshops, science labs, and building challenges led by real scientists and educators.
  • Live Demonstrations & Talks – From mini rocket launches to robotics demos, there’s always something happening at the museum.
  • Special Events – Join us for themed days like Space Day, Dino World, Electricity Explorers, and Science Lab for unforgettable experiences.
  • School & Group Visits – We welcome schools, homeschoolers, and youth groups for customized educational visits and field trips.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to making science engaging and relevant to the lives of young people. Our mission is to:

  • Spark curiosity and a love for science
  • Encourage critical thinking and creativity
  • Support STEM education through hands-on learning
  • Create a welcoming environment for all visitors

    • We believe science is for everyone—regardless of background, ability, or age.

Our Team

Our passionate team includes scientists, educators, artists, and engineers who work together to make science fun and accessible. Whether leading a lab experiment or guiding a planetarium tour, our staff is here to inspire and support every visitor's learning journey.
