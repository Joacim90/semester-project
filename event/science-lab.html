Science Lab

Discover, Experiment, and Explore!

Step into the Science Lab, a thrilling hands-on experience at the Community Science Museum, specially designed for curious minds aged 7–15. This exciting event gives kids the chance to become real scientists for a day—conducting fun, safe experiments, exploring scientific concepts, and uncovering the magic of discovery.

Whether they love bubbling beakers, microscopes, or solving puzzles, young visitors will be immersed in a world of exploration that makes learning come alive.

What to Expect at Science Lab

Mix, measure, and observe! Kids can perform safe and exciting science experiments with guidance from our trained staff. From color-changing reactions to slime-making, each station introduces core scientific principles in a fun and engaging way.

  • Microscope Station: Take a closer look! View insects, plant cells, and everyday objects under microscopes and learn how scientists study the hidden details of our world.
  • Chemical Reactions Zone: Watch things fizz, foam, and pop in a supervised area focused on the science of reactions. Learn about states of matter, acids and bases, and more.
  • Brain Games & Science Puzzles: Challenge your critical thinking skills with science-themed puzzles, riddles, and logic games—perfect for team play or solo fun.
  • Meet Real Scientists: Chat with scientists and lab professionals who’ll explain what they do, show cool demonstrations, and answer kids' burning science questions!

Why Join Us?

Participating in Science Lab will help kids:

  • Gain hands-on experience in a real science lab environment
  • Learn the basics of chemistry, biology, and physics through play
  • Encourage curiosity, observation, and critical thinking
  • Enjoy a safe, supervised, and highly interactive event

Event Details
