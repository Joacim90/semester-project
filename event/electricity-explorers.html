Electricity Explorers

Unleash Your Inner Inventor!

Welcome to Electricity Explorers, an electrifying museum activity designed for kids aged 7–15. Dive into the fascinating world of electricity through hands-on experiments and interactive learning experiences that spark curiosity and power up young minds.

What to Expect

Electricity Explorers offers a variety of engaging activities, including:

  • Interactive Experiments:Build simple circuits, power lights, and explore static electricity in a safe, supervised environment.
  • Educational Exhibits: Discover how electricity works—from batteries to power grids—with exciting, kid-friendly displays.
  • Expert Talks: Hear from scientists and educators who explain the wonders of energy and how we use it every day.
  • Group Challenges: Team up to solve energy puzzles and engineer creative electric inventions.

Why Join Us?

Participating in Electricity Explorers will help kids:

  • Understand the basic principles of electricity and energy.
  • Build problem-solving and engineering skills.
  • Learn through doing in a safe, fun environment.
  • Spark a lifelong interest in science and innovation.
