Visit Us

Step into a world of discovery — and it’s absolutely free!

The Community Science Museum is a vibrant, hands-on learning space dedicated to sparking curiosity and encouraging exploration for visitors of all ages. Whether you’re a parent looking for an exciting day out with your children, a school group seeking an enriching educational experience, or simply someone with a passion for science and discovery — our doors are open to everyone, and general admission is completely free.

Our mission is to make science accessible, engaging, and fun. That’s why we believe no one should be left out of the experience due to cost. From interactive exhibits and live demonstrations to themed science zones, discovery labs, and rotating events, you’re invited to explore without ever needing to purchase a ticket for entry.

Where to Find Us

Community Science Museum

123 Discovery Lane

Wonderville, ST 45678

We’re located in the heart of Wonderville, just a short walk from public transport and easily accessible by car or bike. Whether you're local or visiting from out of town, getting here is simple and convenient.

Museum Hours

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Saturday and Sunday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Closed on major public holidays

Feel free to drop by during opening hours — no reservation needed for general admission! Group visits and special programs can be scheduled in advance.

Admission Policy

Entry to the museum is free for everyone.

That includes access to:

  • All permanent exhibitions
  • Interactive science zones
  • Kid-friendly labs and learning spaces
  • Public lectures and daily demonstrations
  • Community gallery and discovery play areas

We occasionally offer special exhibitions, workshops, and planetarium shows that may require a small fee to cover materials or guest speakers. When that happens, full pricing details are available on our Events page.
