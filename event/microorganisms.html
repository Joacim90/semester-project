Microorganisms

Discover the Hidden World of Tiny Life!

Welcome to the Microorganisms exhibit—an exciting museum experience designed for kids aged 7–15. Get up close (really close!) with bacteria, viruses, fungi, and other microscopic life forms that shape our world in ways you’ve never imagined.

What to Expect

At the Microorganisms exhibit, kids will dive into a fascinating world with:

  • Microscope Stations: View real samples and see live microorganisms in action—watch bacteria swim and fungi grow!
  • Interactive Displays: Touchscreens and animations explain how microbes live, multiply, and help (or harm) us.
  • Hands-On Labs: Try safe, fun experiments like growing mold, swabbing surfaces, and building simple models of cells.
  • Microbe Role-Play Zone: Step into the shoes of a virus or bacteria in games that teach how infections spread—and how to stop them!

Why It’s Awesome

Through this exhibit, kids will:

  • Learn about the vital roles microbes play in our bodies, food, and the environment.
  • Understand how illnesses spread and how hygiene helps prevent them.
  • Build curiosity about biology, health, and ecosystems.
  • Have fun exploring a world they can’t normally see!
