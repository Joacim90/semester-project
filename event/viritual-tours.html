Virtual Tours

Explore the Wonders of Science—Anywhere, Anytime!

Welcome to Virtual Tours, an innovative museum experience designed for kids aged 7–15. Step into the future of exploration with interactive, online journeys through science, history, and discovery—no travel required!

What to Expect

Virtual Tours offers a wide range of exciting digital adventures, including:

  • Interactive 3D Exhibits: Navigate through museum galleries using immersive technology and discover science topics in detail.
  • Live Guided Tours: Join expert-led sessions streamed from real exhibits, with live Q&A and fun facts along the way.
  • Virtual Activities: Complete science challenges, solve puzzles, and unlock badges through engaging online tasks.
  • Behind-the-Scenes Access: Get a peek at areas of the museum not open to the public—like labs and artifact storage!

Why Join Us?

Participating in Virtual Tours will help kids:

  • Stay engaged with science from home or school.
  • Develop digital literacy and curiosity about the world.
  • Explore museum content at their own pace or live with a guid
  • Enjoy a flexible, fun, and educational experience.
